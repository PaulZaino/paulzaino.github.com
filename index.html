About Me


Hello, and thank you for visiting my portfolio. My name is Paul and I recently graduated from UCSD with a BS in Cognitive Science specializing in Human Computer Interaction. I graduated Cum Laude and the curriculum I studied focused on learning how people think, learn, and communicate. The HCI component of my studies introduced me to how I could integrate this knowledge along with user centered design methods to create seamless interaction between humans and computers. This multidisciplinary background gives me an advantage over the pure graphic designer because I have studied information architecture, process flows, site mapping, and how to create wireframes.
